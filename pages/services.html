Our Services

Comprehensive tools and information for our consumers.

Citizen’s Charter

We believe in transparency and ease of use. Our Citizen’s Charter outlines our commitment to you, providing a step-by-step roadmap for system navigation, data input guidelines, and a clear definition of user rights and responsibilities.

Payment Information

While Clear Water Metrics provides comprehensive bill estimations to help you plan your finances, please note that actual payments are handled directly by the Sta. Rosa Water District. Our system serves as your financial planning tool, while official transactions remain within the district’s authorized payment channels.

Consumer Page

Take control of your utility costs through your personalized dashboard. Here, you can review your entire consumption history, compare weekly inputs, and view projected monthly totals. It’s your water usage, visualized.

FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

How do I read my physical meter?

Our guide provides a step-by-step process to help you accurately read your meter and input the data into our system.

Why is there a discrepancy in my estimation?

Estimations are based on the data provided and the current rates. Minor discrepancies can occur due to rounding or recent rate adjustments by the district.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to ensure your personal and usage data is protected at all times.
