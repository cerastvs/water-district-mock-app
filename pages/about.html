About Us

Providing real-time insights into your water consumption.

Company Profile

Clear Water Metrics is a dedicated web-based platform tailored specifically for the residents of the Sta. Rosa Water District. Our mission is to bridge the gap between utility providers and households by providing real-time insights into water consumption. By offering accurate estimations and tracking tools, we empower our community to budget effectively and eliminate the stress of "bill shock."

Water Sources

Our data reflects the supply managed by the Sta. Rosa Water District, which primarily draws from local groundwater reservoirs and certified municipal systems. Clear Water Metrics allows you to monitor exactly how much you are drawing from these local resources, fostering a more mindful approach to our community’s shared water supply.

Privacy Statement

Your trust is our priority. We collect only the essential data required to provide accurate tracking—specifically your account details and meter readings. All information is encrypted and stored securely; we never share your data with third parties without explicit consent. Your information is used solely to enhance your tracking experience and provide bill estimations.
